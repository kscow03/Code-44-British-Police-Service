Recruitment & Selection

Join the Armed Response Unit

The Armed Response Unit seeks professional, motivated and capable officers who demonstrate commitment to operational excellence, teamwork and public service. Recruitment is conducted throughout the year based on operational requirements.

Why Join ARU?

  • Specialist firearms training
  • Career progression opportunities
  • Leadership development pathways
  • Participation in high-profile operations
  • Work alongside highly trained officers

Selection Standards

All applicants are assessed against the professional and operational standards expected of Armed Response Unit personnel.

  • Professionalism and conduct
  • Decision making under pressure
  • Teamwork and communication
  • Activity and commitment levels
  • Tactical knowledge and development
  • Leadership potential

Selection Pathway

Expression of Interest → Mentorship → Selection Process → Firearms Training → Operational Deployment
