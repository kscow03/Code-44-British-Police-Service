Full-Time Operator Application

The Armed Response Unit is seeking professional and dedicated officers for permanent assignment as Full-Time Operators. Successful applicants will become part of a specialist team responsible for delivering armed policing capabilities and supporting high-risk operations.

What We Expect

  • Professional conduct at all times
  • Strong communication skills
  • Consistent activity and attendance
  • Commitment to training and development
  • Ability to work effectively within a team
  • Mature decision-making under pressure

Training Commitment

All successful applicants will be required to complete initial firearms training and maintain operational competence through regular continuation training and exercises.

  • Initial Operator Training
  • Scenario-Based Exercises
  • Operational Assessments
  • Continued Professional Development

Selection Process

  1. Application Submission
  2. Command Review
  3. Interview / Assessment
  4. Training Phase
  5. Operational Qualification

Before You Apply

Applications should only be submitted by officers who are genuinely committed to specialist firearms policing and who can dedicate the time required for training, operational deployments and professional development.
