Armed Response Unit (ARV)

Unit Overview

The Armed Response Unit provides specialist firearms capability in response to high-risk incidents, supporting frontline policing and maintaining public safety.

Core Responsibilities

  • Armed intervention at critical incidents
  • Support to unarmed units
  • High-risk suspect containment
  • Rapid tactical deployment

Primary Objectives

  • Protect life and prevent serious harm
  • Provide an immediate armed response capability
  • Support specialist operations across the force
  • Maintain operational readiness at all times

Operational Capability

  • Authorised Firearms Officers (AFOs)
  • Operational Firearms Commanders (OFCs)
  • Tactical Firearms Command Support
  • Advanced Tactical Resources

Operational Notice

All deployments are conducted in accordance with force policy, command direction and operational necessity.
