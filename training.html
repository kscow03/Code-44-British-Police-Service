Training Division

Training Philosophy

The Armed Response Unit maintains a culture of continuous professional development. Officers are expected to train regularly, maintain operational standards and demonstrate sound decision-making under pressure.

Core Training Areas

  • Firearms Handling & Safety Procedures
  • Use of Force & Decision Making
  • Tactical Movement & Building Entry
  • Vehicle-Based Tactics
  • Scenario-Based Training Exercises
  • Command & Communication Skills

Continuation Training

All Authorised Firearms Officers undertake regular continuation training to ensure skills remain current and operational standards are maintained. Training focuses on both individual competence and team effectiveness.

Development Opportunities

  • Authorised Firearms Officer (AFO)
  • Operational Firearms Commander (OFC)
  • Specialist Tactical Roles
  • Instructor Development Pathways
  • Leadership & Command Development
  • Taser Training Team

Professional Standards

The Training Division promotes accountability, professionalism and operational excellence. Officers are expected to uphold the highest standards both during training and operational deployments.

Operational Notice

Detailed lesson plans, training packages and specialist course material are maintained within the Internal Database and are available to authorised personnel only.
