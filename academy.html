Academy Overview

Welcome to the Code 44 Police Service Armed Response Unit Academy. The Academy delivers the complete training pathway for new Authorised Firearms Officers, ensuring every recruit meets the professional standards expected before operational deployment. Training is delivered through classroom sessions, practical exercises, scenario assessments and instructor mentoring.
